AI Chatbots

Replika/Studio-d-id

Replika

Replika has a lot of features to use I guess just for fun. The bot builds a connection with you based on your profile information and it keeps the chat history too. The app added almost all features that a real person is able to use on messengers. This may help users interact with the bot as someone else and thus, leading to feel that they are socializing. The language it uses is also not that formal. However, I did not find it natural as it does not make any mistakes when writing or remembering my information.

I’m not sure why normal people would use this bot except just for fun. But language learners might find this useful to interact with as they can express any thought without a fear of making a mistake, but the bot is still not the only option in this as I’ve seen people doing IELTS speaking practice with tools like Chatgpt. I wonder how Premium users are even paying for this bot just to entertain themselves.

Studio-d-id

I felt like Studio-d-id is a visualized version of tools like Chatgpt, Gemini, but with less functionalities. Responses here are much shorter compared with those two. It is natural as they created a talking AI and it would be strange if those talks were long and enriched like GPT or Gemini. They made agents available in various languages. I tried English and Turkish and I loved it as they talk like a real native.

Some cool stuff is available for only premium users. Integrating an agent to your app is not an exception. Just like Replika, language learners may find this app useful. However, they can be used for a variety of purposes just by integrating them to one’s app or a website. They might be used to help people find out about a website’s functionalities, navigate through the website, simply give information on whatever the users are curious about.
