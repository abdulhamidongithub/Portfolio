AI Presentations

Slidesgo/Gamma/Presentations.ai

Slidesgo Presentation

Pros: User-friendly interface. I liked it as it has other services like lesson-planning and quizzes. Incredibly fast to generate presentations. You can choose one of the 4-5 options for the design and it made a cool one I guess. The best part is that you can change anything you want just like you do on a PPT file.

Cons: You cannot write your expectations about the content as it gives you a space for the title only. The presentation covered the topic to some extent, however, I did not see any specifications or examples related to the topic. The topic was “Gamification in Leetcode’s case”. It mentioned badges and competitions as examples in general, but did not name any specific ones.

Gamma Presentation

Gamma gives more freedom as you can either give your notes about the presentation or just the title only based on your preference. This allows you to include your own thoughts, examples from the very get-go. You have more options to customize the design, colors and the fonts. AI image generation also looks great. Plus, I liked the way it allows you to choose the types of images to use such as AI-images or images from free sources! Gamma did a little bit better job with the content as it tried to generate an image that just looks like the image from the Leetcode platform itself, but you can notice that it is AI as some letters are confusing.

Also, compared to the other two, Gamma gives you more options even when exporting your work. You can either create a public link for different purposes or download it in different formats. The content coverage also seems wider compared with slidesgo. I have seen examples, some numbers to make the thoughts in it more reliable.

Presentations.ai

I see almost the same features in presentations.ai with gamma.app. I find the design much more creative compared with gamma and slidesgo. The design looks classic, but in some modern way. I have no idea how to describe it properly. It is simple, but appealing at the same time(unlike the one from slidesgo). Variety of structures are used to deliver the information in this app. But compared with gamma, it does not allow you to input much longer texts to describe your topic.

The only problem I came across with this app is that it did not allow me to export my work as a single file on a free plan. It kept asking me to pay for it. And even the embedded link did not work to show the presentation here on my website.

Conclusion

Slidesgo.com. The content generated seems too general to me. I guess it lacks creativity in design compared with the other two as it used the same template twice in my case. I chose different options in two cases though. I would use it if there are no other options and I’m short on time.

Gamma.app. It gave me more detailed content with examples, specifications. I liked the design as it used different types of layouts to deliver the information. I’d use it in any case as I’m not good at designs. I guess I cannot do a presentation this good so I’d prefer to provide the requirements and ask this app to do it for me.

Presentations.ai. The same opinions with the ones about gamma.app. However, unlike gamma, the free plan is very limited when it comes to exporting the output for future use.

Overall, I would definitely use one of these apps to create presentations as I do not have a good taste in design. Not only the design, they also help you with the organization of your thoughts or even content generation although you will have to add some specific thoughts and examples. And they do all of these in just 2-3 minutes.
